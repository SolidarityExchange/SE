How to use these lists

Pick a retailer below and purchase items directly from the wishlist. If checkout asks for an address, it should auto‑fill. Keep your receipt just in case.

Tip: Smaller, practical items tend to be approved faster.

Heads up about rejections

Facilities sometimes reject care packages (rules, vendor limits, or content). This is especially common with "bad‑boy" books or anything the mailroom considers edgy. Check the facility's rules before buying.

  • Prefer new items shipped by the retailer (avoid third‑party sellers where possible).
  • No hardcovers where prohibited; avoid spiral bindings if restricted.
  • Avoid explicit content or how‑to violence/exploits.

If something is rejected, request a refund from the retailer and tell us so we can adjust the list.

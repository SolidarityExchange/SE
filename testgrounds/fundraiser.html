Support Incarcerated Comrades

Browse our Prisoner Registry to find individuals you'd like to support. You can contribute in two ways: use our proxy service for anonymous giving, or make direct purchases through retailer links and commissary systems.


For further clarification, review our wishlist instructions.

Most inmates are able to recieve gifts as well as commissary funds:


  • Wishlist Fulfillment: Some prisoners maintain public or proxy wishlists (Amazon, Target, Walmart). Browse profiles in the Prisoner Registry for individual links. As stated before, you can contribute to wishlists anonymously.
  • Commissary Contributions: Most state and federal prisons allow direct money transfers. Check the Prisoner Registry page to learn the name of the facility your prisoner is at, and follow their commissary instructions. Feel free to email us if you're unsure or use our proxy commissary service instead.

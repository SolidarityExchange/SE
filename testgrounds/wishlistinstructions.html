Anonymous/Pseudonymous Donation System via The Solidarity Exchange

To contribute items while protecting your privacy, follow these steps:

  1. Select the items you wish to purchase from the prisoner's wishlist
  2. Note the Item Codes for each item (a, b, c, etc.)
  3. Contact us at [email address] with the Item Codes and prisoner name. Failure to do so may create issues.
  4. We will provide payment instructions for the total amount
  5. Once payment is received, we will purchase and ship the items on your behalf
​

Direct Purchase Guidelines

If you prefer to purchase items directly, please follow these guidelines:

  • Ensure the item complies with the facility's acceptance policies.
  • Be sure to use the proper address format, which typically includes a name and a prisoner ID.
  • Include your return address in case the item is rejected
  • Note that your Name and other information may be accessible to the inmate and facility. Typically, this is restricted to your name and the city you live in.
  • Keep your order confirmation and tracking information
​

Important Notes

  • All items must be shipped directly from the retailer, which we have deliberately selected to show you.
  • Books must be paperback only - hardcovers are typically rejected
  • No used items, food, or homemade goods are accepted
  • Delivery may take 2-4 weeks depending on facility processing

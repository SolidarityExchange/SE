Support Incarcerated Comrades

Each individual listed on our registry has unique needs—from hygiene items and stamps to legal supplies and food. We offer two main ways to support:

  • Wishlist Fulfillment: Some prisoners maintain public or proxy wishlists (Amazon, Target, Walmart). Browse profiles in the Prisoner Registry for individual links.
  • Commissary Contributions: If a wishlist isn't available, most state and federal prisons allow direct money transfers. Visit our Verification Tools page to locate state-specific deposit instructions, or email us if you're unsure.

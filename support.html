Supporting Prisoners

Supporting prisoners starts with finding accurate information. Below are some basic steps and resources to locate and assist incarcerated individuals.

Finding Federal Prisoners

You can search for individuals in federal prisons using the Federal Inmate Locator.

Finding State Prisoners

Each state has its own inmate lookup system. Click here for a list of state prisoner lookup tools (coming soon).

Finding County Jail Detainees

For county jails, you may need to check the jailâ€™s official website. Search online for the county name and "inmate lookup" or "jail roster." Many county jails have online search tools.

Using VINELink as a 'catch-all'

VINELink is a national service that allows you to locate most state and local prisoners as well as some federal prisoners. You can also receive notifications about their custody status. You can access it at VINELink.com.

Writing to Prisoners

One of the best ways to support incarcerated individuals is by sending them letters. A simple letter can provide encouragement, solidarity, and connection to the outside world.

We will be launching an email-to-letter proxy service that allows you to send messages to prisoners without revealing your personal address. Stay tuned for updates!

The Solidarity Exchange exists to provide support to incarcerated Whites across the globe who continue to support their own, and are ideologically opposed to Jewish power, without any other qualifications whatsoever.

Many of these prisoners would likely have never been prosecuted if the Judeo-American Empire did not exist, and would likely have been an uninterrupted benefit to society at-large. Similarly, under a fair system, others may have been less severely punished had their ideology not represented the morally superior inversion of the current power system.

Like any population within America, some of SE’s listed prisoners have been caught up in apolitical street crime, and their allegiance to their extended White family is not directly related to their incarceration.

On the other hand, some of the very same have been accused of very serious crimes, with credible evidence suggesting a deliberate, and extremely misguided intent to use violence to further their racial ideology.

For the purposes of the Solidarity Exchange, none of this matters.

Like its name says, The Solidarity Exchange is a network that promotes solidarity with incarcerated Whites who are National Socialist, or National Socialist adjacent.

Currently, SE provides various services in order to accomplish this. This includes the maintenance of a [[[database]]] of prisoner mailing addresses and biographies. Additionally, privacy-wary users are encouraged to make use of our [[[letter proxy service]]], where anyone wishing to acquire a pen pal can do so without revealing their mailing address.

The Solidarity Exchange also provides other services, including but not limited to:

  • [[[The Solidarity Dispatch]]], a periodical newsletter available for free to prisoners as well as visitors to our [[[Web site.]]]
  • Instructions for contributing to a prisoner’s commissary fund, allowing them to purchase essential items like toothbrushes as well as snacks and other ‘luxuries.’
  • A space for the promotion of fundraisers for the incarcerated or their loved ones.

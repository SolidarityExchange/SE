The mission of The Solidarity Exchange is to facilitate material and moral support to incarcerated whites who remain loyal to their heritage, oppose Jewish supremacism, and align with a National Socialist ethic.


We extend solidarity without conditions.



The Solidarity Exchange operates under the reality of the Judeo-American Empire—a regime that enforces anti-white policies, manipulates justice for political ends, and deliberately fractures our people. Many of the individuals listed here would never have been imprisoned, or would have faced far lesser sentences, under a system that did not criminalize their beliefs.


Some have been convicted of ordinary street crimes, while others stand accused of politically motivated, and sometimes violent, acts. Regardless of the charges, we recognize that the current power structure specifically targets whites. Therefore, The Solidarity Exchange does not involve itself in legal debates—we focus on ensuring that our people, once behind bars, are not forgotten.


Current services include:


  • A registry of prisoner profiles and mailing addresses.
  • A secure letter proxy for anonymous correspondence.
  • The Solidarity Dispatch, a free newsletter available for prisoners as well as the general public.
  • Resources for contributing to commissary funds and legal aid.
  • A platform for fundraising to assist prisoners and their families.

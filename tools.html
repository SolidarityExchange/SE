Verification Tools

Use the resources below to confirm current addresses, inmate status, or case details before donating or writing.


Search Tip: When using court records, middle names, approximate ages, and sentencing documents (if available) can help you confirm you’ve found the correct person—especially for common names. Match case details to incarceration timelines for added certainty.

Federal Tools

  • BOP Inmate Locator – Find people in federal custody by name or ID number.
  • VINE Link – Tracks custody status for inmates in many state and county systems.
  • PACER – View federal court records (most users won’t be billed if under ~$30/quarter or 250–300 pages).
    We support RECAP, a free archive that mirrors documents shared by PACER users.

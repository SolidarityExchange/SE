Email-to-Letter Proxy Service

Supporters can now write to incarcerated individuals through our proxy mailing system. Here's how it works:

  • Send an email to letters@yourdomain.org
  • Use the subject line to include the prisoner's name or ID
  • Write your message in the body of the email
  • We'll print and mail it out within 7 days

Privacy: We do not include your email address in the letter unless requested. If you'd like the recipient to write back, include your mailing address.

We currently do not accept attachments, images, or greeting cards. Letters may be reviewed before mailing to ensure they comply with prison mail policies.
